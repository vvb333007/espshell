Vyacheslav Logunov

Born in 1978. Graduated from the Moscow Institute of Electronic Engineering (MIEE ) with a degree in electronic engineering.

Completed internships at the Research Institute of Microdevices in Zelenograd, as well as at TrustWorks B.V., a company developing solutions based on IPSEC and IKE protocols.

Worked at ZELAX in the software department, where I developed system software for IP routers. Implemented support for IPSec, NAT, Telnet, SSH, IP traffic filtering, Multilink PPP, and drivers for Ethernet and ATM, among many other features.

Familiar with PowerPC, Xtensa, and RISC-V architectures. Developed Linux drivers and created Board Support Packages (BSP) for RTEMS.

Interests: electronics, programming, scuba diving, natural sciences
